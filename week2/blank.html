As We May Think

“As We May Think” by Vannevar Bush highlights how future generations could possibly process and store information. Having written the article in 1945, It’s impressive to see how many advanced pieces of technology have come to life. The evolution of high-quality cameras is spot-on, mentioning how we continue to improve the lens, the quality, as well as other aspects.

Also, the “memex” is an exact blueprint of the modern web where records, communications, and information can be efficiently mechanized. This article puts into perspective the evolution of technology and how far we’ve advanced in terms of practicality and information gathering. .
