Response to Auriea Harvey and
the Future of Digital Sculpture

Aureia Harvey is a digital artist/sculptor who coded websites as art and listed them on the internet. Her early childhood consisted of messing around with code in magazines and debugging in the included programs.




The fact that she saw the opportunity to learn html when the internet just came out is very impressive. With her proactiveness, she was able to find many jobs and opportunities to pursue her passion. She also did this during a time period when girls working with computers were something out of the ordinary.



She says that she wishes for future artists to work purely digitally and make a living in the same way as other artists. I admire her compassion and the way she wants to inspire future generations. homepage
