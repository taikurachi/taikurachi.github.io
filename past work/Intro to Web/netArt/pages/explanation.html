The Dollie Clones are a collection of two telerobotic dolls based on Lynn Hershman Leeson herself and her alter ego, Roberta Breitmore. CybeRoberta is a miniature replica of Roberta Breitmore and Tillie the Telerobotic Doll, a doll version of Leeson, resembles her evil twin.
These toys catered toward children are designed like cyborgs and have cameras as their eyes. When these twins are exhibited in galleries, people can see the world through their perspective using a live video feed or on their designated website where updated images are posted every thirty seconds. Additionally, online viewers can control the direction of the dolls' gaze by clicking on the images of their eyes, allowing them to explore their surroundings.
The Dollie Clones address various topics that Hershman Leeson has been interested in exploring throughout her career, including surveillance, the malleability of identity, clones and cyborgs, and the representation of gender and the self.
